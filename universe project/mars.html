MARS
  • Mars is the fourth planet from the sun.
  • Its average distance from the sun is about 227.9 million kilometers.
  • It takes 687 days to complete its orbit.
  • Mars has two tiny moond called Phobos and Deimos.
  • Phobos is 27 km across while Deimos is just 15 km across.
  • The red surface on mars is because of the presense of iron on its surface.
